K-Pop

I was heavily into J-Pop which was a direct result of being such an anime fan in my youth. I was slowly introduced to Korean pop music because of a particular artist that was very famous in the early 2000's; BoA. In 2009, my favourite J-Pop artist retired (only for a few years) and this event broke my fandom's heart. However, BoA released a very, very strong album with a more mature sound and it quickly snowballed into all-out fandom for her works and then, the rest of K-Pop came.
