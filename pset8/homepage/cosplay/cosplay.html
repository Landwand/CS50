Cosplay: a hobby of dressing up as a character

I don't know when exactly I started to make costumes, but I would imagine that it's sometime around the early 2000's after attending my first anime convention as a very shy person and not knowing how to make conversation. At the time, I had proficiency in making Gundam and similar models, so prop making was a natural extension of that. Yet sewing and wigwork were completely different creatures and my resulting early costumes are embarassing.
